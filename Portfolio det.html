PROVISIONS BRANDING

A Necessary Brand

Shoe boutique, P.V.S, is an acronym for Provisions. Inspired by the European, English and American Groceries & Provisions shops of the 1930’s–1950’s, the brand represents footwear which not only grounds itself in the enduring roots of heritage but serves as a reminder that shoes are a necessity, and not a mere fashion accessory.

True to its Label

In tribute to the heritage of service excellence that was exemplary of these provision shops, P.V.S bears its own trademark with beautifully designed name cards, paper bags, note pads, envelopes and cash vouchers. These are all embellished with a colour theme and motifs, inspired by traditional labels and receipts of the past.
